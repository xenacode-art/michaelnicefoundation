About Us

Magret Annums-Uzomaka is the pionner and founder of Michael-Nice Foundattion. The name emanated from the sudden death of her beloved brother Angel Michael, Who was a passionate advoacte for the downtrodden,Widows,Fatherless and for Justice. The residue of his legacy will continually last through this mission he passed to us.

An Overview

Michael-Nice Foundation is a dream thatt came from the loss of a dear brother,and how he lived to help those in need. The organisation was registered in 2018 in Houston Texas,USA.

We address social barriers to economic growth by providing financial assistance to alleviate sufferings of the under-privileged.
